Milky Way is our one and only cat.
She is considered an emotional support animal.
The companionship that she provides helps with anxiety and depression.

Personality:

Milky Way is a somewhat fickle cat.
She can both be a very lazy kitty or a playful kitty.
She can be very affectionate when she wants to be.
She loves to snuggle up with us in bed at night.
She isn't very vocal and usually only meows when she wants attention or food.
She is very independent even though she prefers to be around us.
She gets along well with other cats and dogs but doesn't like grabby toddlers.
